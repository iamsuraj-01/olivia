My Approach

How I Work and Support You

I Attend to Your Mind

I hear you, I hear the conflict and the overwhelm of a preoccupied mind stuck in its story. I am often told I detangle thoughts and put everything in order. I clarify, I educate, I organize, so you can leave feeling lighter, clearer, more self-aware, and more empowered to change what no longer works for you.

I Listen to Your Body

There is what you share in words and what is said through you. I read you and attune deeply to your emotional body so I can hear all that is not being said. My approach is also to utilize your senses, your nervous system, your hormones, and your subtle body language to gain deeper insights.

I Talk to Your Soul

As you enter the space, I call for the most whole and healed version of yourself. I look at the bigger picture and sense what is most needed at this time, beyond the idea of what problem you brought to solve. This allows you to leave having resolved what will create the most significant change on your journey.
